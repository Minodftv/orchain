AI Marketplace

The DINO AI Marketplace provides high-quality, validated AI solutions for businesses, with on-chain payment and plug-and-play integration via APIs.

OWallet

Multi-chain non-custodial wallet with interoperability in mind, compatible with IBC, EVM and TVM networks.

OraiDEX

CosmWasm-based AMM with multi-chain interoperability, optimal speed, and lots of liquidity options.

OBridge

Expanding interoperability by connecting liquidity and messaging between IBC, EVM and TVM networks.

Distilled AI

Distilled AI goes beyond theory to deliver value, providing specialized and personalized models that power best-in-class applications, alleviate pain points for real-world businesses, and provide consumers with robust personal agents that preserve private personality.

DINO Hub

Data and AI Marketplace. Tooling for data scientists to train, test and publish models to Oraichain's AI blockchain oracle.

CosmWasm IDE

Developer environment for Cosmos network dApp development with code editor, compiler, debugger, and deployment tool.

EUENO

Decentralized storage and sharing platform for Web3 projects, offering end-to-end encryption, serverless hosting and competitive pricing.

KawaiiQ

KawaiiQ is a cutting-edge parenting app that combines educational games for kids with comprehensive support for parents. It offers essential resources and AI-driven features for confident parenting and fostering children’s development—all within a secure environment.

aiRight

NFT Marketplace with many AI-generated media models and authenticity reporting by default.

Modestus

Modestus seamlessly moderates content, analyzes interactions, and creates a secure environment, allowing users to engage confidently and gain valuable knowledge.

Trava

TRAVA is a decentralized cross-chain lending marketplace with user-created lending pools and credit score function. It prioritizes risk management and security.

BlockAI.Care

BlockAI.Care is a smart healthcare dApp that uses AI to provide affordable and accurate pre-diagnosis and health monitoring.

Kucoin

KuCoin is a popular centralized exchange for buying ORAI tokens, supporting deposit and withdrawal for BSC, ETH and Oraichain native tokens.

Gate.io

Gate.io is a secure exchange for trading cryptocurrencies. ORAI token can be deposited or withdrawn only through Ethereum.

Oraichain VRF

Oraichain VRF 2.0 uses on-chain verification and a threshold cryptography system to provide fully decentralized, verifiable and bias-resistant random values.

Ziden

Self-sovereign identity solutions for a decentralized identity ecosystem, supporting verifications with zero-knowledge proofs.

Data Marketplace

The DINO Data Marketplace enables users to contribute and monetize data-related tasks such as data labeling, dataset creation, and label requests for businesses and other users.

Royalty Protocol

Preserving downstream royalties for content creators, Oraichain's Royalty Protocol is a blockchain-based platform ensuring fair compensation for their work.

Originality Check

The NFT Authenticity check verifies on-chain and off-chain content authenticity, detects copyright infringement, and provides transaction history and AI-based reporting.

Orchai

Orchai Liquid Staking is a protocol that allows users to earn and use staked ORAI derivatives as collateral for borrowing.

Neststock

An information and investment gateway for Real estate: research ideal houses, do analysis with AI tools, and smoothly execute transactions.

(Comming soon)

Oraichain Academy

Learn more about Oraichain's vision + technologies through research posts, and editorials and lessons.

yAI.Finance

Yield aggregator for DeFi investors that uses AI-powered services from Oraichain to automate profitable investment portfolios. It offers yield aggregation, DeFi analytics, DeFi portfolio tracking, and a DeFi gaming protocol.

Syscoin

Syscoin builds leading-edge technology that scales blockchain's original core values to meet the demands of a global population.

TRON

TRON is an ambitious project dedicated to building the infrastructure for a truly decentralized Internet.

DoraHacks

DoraHacks is a global hacker movement and the world's most active multi-chain Web3 developer incentive platform.

Netvrk

Netvrk is a multichain metaverse built on Unreal Engine that enables users to socialize, play, create and monetize content.

Rikkeisoft

Rikkeisoft is a leading IT company in Vietnam and a trusted partner for many large enterprises in the Japanese Market.

Imba Games

IMBA is dedicated to creating high-quality and enjoyable games that will stand the test of time.

Astar Network

Astar Network provides the infrastructure for building dApps with EVM and WASM smart contracts offering developers true interoperability with cross-consensus messaging (XCM) and a cross-virtual machine (XVM).

Ocean Protocol

Ocean Protocol makes it easy to publish data services (deploy ERC721 data NFTs and ERC20 datatokens), and to consume data services (spend datatokens).

VAIOT

VAIOT offers a portfolio of blockchain-based AI Assistants and on-chain Intelligent Contracts for businesses and consumers to provide automated services and transactions.

Oraichain is the world's first layer 1 of AI blockchain oracle established in 2020. The project is now driving advancements in human-centric artificial intelligence and cross-chain interoperability, paying the way for next-generation Web3 applications and their mass adoption.
